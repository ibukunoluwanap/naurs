Who We Are

I am Natarajan Pandian, I have 20+ year experience in Music and Media Industry as System Sound Engineer. I started my career at Arabian Radio Network (ARN) from 2001 to 2008 and from the past 10+ year I am with Dubai Media INC (DMI) Dubai Government as Broadcast Sound Engineer. Also I’m a Brown Belt in Budokan Karate and have participated Jr National Body Building India 1998-1999. Basically I have a vast knowledge of Music and Fitness Industries.

Music and Technology is not only my profession but also my passion and I want to implement it as a business in Bay Avenue as a Music and Fitness Center. Bay Avenue – Executive Tower has everything to offer except a Music, dance, art and a Fitness form studio which stand Unique to any other classes around the radius. We as a family are into construction material and Trading business since 30+ year in Oman and in UAE. We are very confident that this would be a successful business and attract those audiences who wants to fulfill their passion as a vocalist, guitarist, Prenatal postnatal yoga, Gravity (swing Yoga) classical dance form and Basic Gymnastic for kids.

Now you may ask why Bay Avenue; It’s a big responsibility for one or parents to drive and find parking and attend class especially its impossible for working parents to drop their kids in a class wait and pick them back considering social distancing whereas the kids and grownups can walk in the Bay Avenue Mall. (Our target audience are residence of Executive Towers. We are planning to implement High tech acoustic studios on F42 & F43 and 11.1.2 Dolby Digital on the unit F44 (yoga, theater, dance practice

We will also be offering free classes to 65+ aged people such as dancing, basic yoga and meditation and other class subject to availability. We believe this will bring happiness, relieve physical and mental stress. Our Target audience are all age groups as we want to build a positive vibe an “Aura”. Our target customers will be all nationality considering that we will be appointing Qualified Teachers and Trainers.
